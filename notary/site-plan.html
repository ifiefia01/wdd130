Content

Main Objective

Fiekai Notary Services provides services to certify and authenticate signatures for many types of loans and documents. Once a lender or financial institution requires documents to be notarized, the lender will require a certified notarized signature in order for the documents to be legally accepted. This site aims to deliver top notch assistance for the understanding and clarity as to why things need to be notarized and the purpose the notary agent serves. This site will be a way for customers to book a notary signing, set an appointment and use online technology to complete signings within the comfort of their own home.

Clarity and Understanding

The information on the site will clarify what is needed by the customer, what the agent will provide and what the agent is allowed to explain. Certain federal regulations prevent the discussion for the agent to explain many topics in a notary setting. So this site will help alleviate any questions that customers may have before their appointment with the notary agent. Customers will be able to request notary services by navigating the site and choosing the type of service that best suits their needs. On the home page, customers will be introduced to the owner and operator of Fiekai Notary Services, along with visuals that will enhance their experience and provide comfort.

Fees/Services

Fees and/or Rates

Each additional page will provide opportunities to see a full notary website. This page will consist of opportunities for the owner to surface all information needed for the customer to make an educated decision and also show the customer competitive pricing. It will alleviate any confusion towards the fee structure for all services. Customers will be able to pick and choose the type of service they require to ensure the completion of their documents. Customers will be assured that the rates they see are within the guidelines for proper notary services and also see that our pricing is competitive and affordable. They will get a better understanding of the process and satisfy their every click.

Services

This site will help customers understand the world of notary services and serve as a knowledgeable way for customers to see the many types of services that are offered. When customers visit the site, they will leave with a better understanding of where they are in their loan process and where they need to be to be able to thoroughly complete the steps necessary for their documents. Customers may be able to complete services online without the need stress over a meeting place. FieKai Notary provides a way to sign online for certain services so that the customer won’t be inconvenienced.
