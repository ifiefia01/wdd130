About Me

My name is Alice Fiefia. I was born and raised in West Valley City, Utah. I have been married since 2005 to my amazing husband and we have 4 children together. Our kids are very active in sports, which makes me a full-time mom as well. Pickups and dropoffs are part of my daily activity along with performing my duties as a Certified Notary. I grew a passion for Notary services when I acquired my certifications. Being able to assist families in their pursuit for a better life inspires me daily and helps me love what I do. Please leave me a message or email me if you have any questions. I am here to help!
